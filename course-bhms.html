Course description

BHMS stands for Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery. It's a 5.5-year undergraduate program offered in India that equips graduates with the knowledge and skills to become qualified homeopathic doctors..

Course Duration-5.5 year program

Application period:June and July.

(4.5 years of classroom study plus 1 year mandatory internship).

  • Educational Background: You must have passed Class 12th from a recognized board in India.
  • 2. Minimum Marks: There's a minimum qualifying percentage requirement, but this can vary across universities. It typically ranges from 50% to 60% for the Science subjects combined. Some universities might have separate minimum scores for each science subject.
  • Age Limit: There might be an age limit for admission. The general range is 17 years old on or before a specific date (usually December 31st of the admission year). Some colleges or universities might offer relaxation for reserved categories like SC/ST.
  • Entrance Exams: Most universities and colleges offering BHMS programs require you to qualify in a national entrance exam like NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test). This is a competitive exam that tests your knowledge in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
Get Expert Guidance.
  • Homeopathic Doctor: This is the primary career path for BHMS graduates. You can set up your own practice, work in homeopathic hospitals and clinics, or join wellness centers.
  • Homeopathic Pharmacist: Become the medicine maker! This role involves understanding the properties of medicinal plants used in homeopathy, formulating remedies based on the Homeopathic Pharmacopoeia, and ensuring safe preparation techniques.
  • Research & Academics: Contribute to the advancement of homeopathic knowledge. Pursue postgraduate studies in specialized areas like homeopathy and mental health, conduct research on the efficacy of homeopathic treatments, or teach future generations of homeopathic practitioners.
  • Develop Homeopathic Products: Combine your homeopathic knowledge with entrepreneurial spirit. Develop innovative homeopathic formulations for specific health concerns.
