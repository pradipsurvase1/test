About us

Take the first step towards your medical ambitions.

Shiv Sangam is India's leading medical entrance counseling center, dedicated to empowering students to achieve their dreams of becoming doctors. With a proven track record of success, we have guided over 10,000 students towards securing placements in top medical colleges across India and abroad.

But our expertise goes beyond just knowledge. We understand the anxieties, aspirations, and unique challenges students face as they navigate this critical phase.
